Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CashTrack (also referred to as "Spendr" in our design framework). We are committed to protecting your personal and financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information within our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless financial tracking experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile photo provided during account creation via Google or Email.
  • Financial Data: Transaction history, categories, and budget limits that you enter manually or are captured via SMS.
  • SMS Metadata: If you grant permission, the app parses bank-related SMS messages locally on your device to extract transaction details.

3. Local-First Processing

We prioritize your privacy by adopting a Local-First architecture. Most of your financial data is stored securely on your device using an encrypted Room database. We do not upload your raw financial records to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud synchronization.

4. AI Insights & Gemini

CashTrack uses the Google Gemini API to provide intelligent financial advice. When you request AI insights, relevant (anonymized) transaction summaries are sent to Google's servers for processing. This data is transmitted via encrypted HTTPS tunnels and is used solely to generate your personalized advice.

5. Permissions and Their Use

  • RECEIVE_SMS / READ_SMS: Used exclusively for the "Automated Tracking" feature to capture bank transaction alerts.
  • RECORD_AUDIO: Used for the optional voice-command feature to add expenses hands-free.
  • USE_BIOMETRIC: Used to lock the app behind your device's fingerprint or face recognition for extra security.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including Firebase Authentication and local database encryption, to protect your data. However, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and account credentials.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data at any time. You can clear all local data through the app settings or request account deletion by contacting us at leanbytes0@gmail.com.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to: leanbytes0@gmail.com
