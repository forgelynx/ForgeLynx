About the App

Game Booster Pro & Utilities is your ultimate companion for mobile gaming. It provides a suite of tools to optimize your device's performance, monitor system resources, and enhance your gaming experience.

From blocking notifications to monitoring FPS and Ping, Game Booster Pro ensures you have the competitive edge you need.

Key Features

🚀

Game Boost

Boost & Launch your games at just one click. Optimize system resources for maximum gaming performance.

🔕

Notification Blocker

Block annoying notifications while playing games. Stay focused on your gameplay without interruptions.

📊

FPS Meter

Monitor real-time FPS of your device anywhere on your screen with customizable color, size and position.

📶

Ping Meter

Monitor live network ping/latency of your device anywhere on the screen with all customization options.

💾

Resource Monitor

Monitor the usable RAM and Storage of your device represented in percentage for optimal performance.

🤖

Gemini AI Chatbot

AI Chatbot to help you with your gaming related queries and give suggestions for better gameplay.
