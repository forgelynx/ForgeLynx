About Countdown Timer Widget

Get a beautiful countdown timer widget right on your HOME SCREEN with days until & time left for your special events. This modern countdown app shows time until any event with home screen widgets and a minimalistic design.

Track the remaining days and time until any upcoming events like birthdays, vacations, holidays, marriages, anniversaries, or retirement. The app lets you add countdown timers on your home screen so you don't need to open the app every time you want to check the countdown progress.
