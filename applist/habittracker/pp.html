HabitTracker

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Introduction

At HabitTracker, we believe your habits are a private part of your personal journey. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information with a focus on maximum transparency and user control.

Data Collection & Storage

Local Storage: HabitTracker uses an on-device Room database to store your habits and logs. This data is processed locally to provide fast, offline-first performance.

Cloud Synchronization: We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) for cross-device synchronization. This is an opt-in feature that stores your habit configurations and completion logs on secure servers.

Data Export: You can export your habit history at any time in CSV or JSON format. This export is generated locally on your device.

Technical Transparency

We implement Row Level Security (RLS) at the database level. This technical safeguard ensures that even at the infrastructure layer, your data is isolated and can only be accessed by your authenticated account.

Third-Party Services

To maintain app stability and improve user experience, we utilize:

  • Google Firebase: For anonymous crash reporting (Crashlytics) and basic usage analytics.
  • Supabase: For secure authentication and data synchronization.
  • Google Play Store / Apple App Store: For processing subscription payments.

Subscriptions & Payments

HabitTracker offers a Premium subscription. All financial transactions are handled securely by the respective app store (Google or Apple). We do not collect or store your payment card details or bank information. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status to unlock premium features.

Device Permissions

The app requires the following permissions for its core functionality:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To deliver your scheduled habit reminders.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To automatically reschedule your reminders after your device restarts.
  • INTERNET: To synchronize your data with the cloud (if enabled).

Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual habits or your entire account directly from the app settings. Account deletion triggers a cascade delete on our servers, immediately and permanently removing all your habits and logs from Supabase.
