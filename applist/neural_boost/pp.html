This Privacy Policy describes how Neural Boost (the "App"), developed by Lean Bytes, handles your data. We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring a transparent experience. By using the App, you agree to the practices described herein.

Information Collection & Usage

Neural Boost is designed to be privacy-centric. We do not require account creation, and we do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or physical address.

To provide performance optimization features, the App accesses non-personal technical data including:

  • Device specifications (Model, SoC, RAM capacity).
  • Real-time hardware telemetry (CPU/GPU load, Battery status).
  • System performance metrics (FPS, Network Latency, and Jitter).
  • Package querying (Anonymized list of installed applications to identify games).

Permissions & Functional Rationale

Neural Boost requires specific system permissions to function as an advanced optimizer. Each permission is used exclusively for its stated purpose:

  • Usage Patterns (QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES): Necessary for the Game Launcher to scan and detect installed games for easy access and optimization.
  • Display Over Other Apps (Overlay): Essential for the Smart HUD to render FPS and Ping metrics while you are gaming in other applications.
  • Do Not Disturb (DND Access): Enables "Focus Mode" by automatically managing system notification policies to silence alerts during gameplay.
  • Kill Background Processes: Allows the "One-Tap Boost" feature to terminate idle applications, reclaiming fragmented RAM for your game.
  • Foreground Service: Ensures the Neural Engine remains stable and provides real-time optimization even when the app is in the background.
  • Network State: Used to calculate ping/latency and monitor the stability of your gaming connection.
  • Post Notifications: Required on Android 13+ to send critical status alerts and performance reports.
