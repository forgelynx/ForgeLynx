Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

Focus on Privacy

Welcome to Serene Interval Countdown. We believe privacy is the foundation of peace. This app is designed to respect your space and your data.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Serene Interval Countdown operates entirely on your device. We do not have access to your countdown history, labels, or usage patterns.

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function smoothly:

  • Notifications: Used to signal the end of a countdown or interval.
  • Exact Alarms: Necessary for precision timing, ensuring your intervals are accurate down to the millisecond.
  • Background Service: Allows the timer to continue running while you focus on other tasks or dim your screen.

Analytics & Ads

This app contains no advertising, no tracking SDKs, and no hidden analytics. Your time is yours alone.

Third Parties

Since we do not collect data, we have nothing to share with third parties. Your data never leaves the encrypted sandbox of your mobile device.
