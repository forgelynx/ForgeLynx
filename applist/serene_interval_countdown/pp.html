Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

Focus on Privacy

Welcome to Serene Interval Countdown. We believe privacy is the foundation of peace. This app is designed to respect your space and your data.

Data We Collect

To provide authentication and account functionality, Serene Interval Countdown collects the following personal information when you create an account:

  • Name: Your display name, used to personalize your experience within the app.
  • Email Address: Used for account authentication, login, and essential account-related communications.
  • Profile Photo: A photo you provide during sign-up, used as your profile avatar within the app.

We do not collect or have access to your countdown history, interval labels, or usage patterns. All timer-related data remains entirely on your device.

How We Use Your Data

Your personal information is used solely for authentication and account management purposes. Specifically:

  • To verify your identity when you sign in.
  • To display your name and profile photo within the app.
  • To communicate critical account-related information (e.g., password resets).

Data Security

Important: Please be aware that your personal data (name, email, and profile photo) is not encrypted in transit. This means that information transmitted between your device and our servers may be vulnerable to interception. We recommend avoiding the use of sensitive or confidential information in your profile. We are actively working to implement encryption for data in transit in a future update.

Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any third parties. Your name, email, and profile photo are used exclusively within Serene Interval Countdown and are never disclosed to external services, advertisers, or partners.

Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of all your personal data at any time. To request data deletion:

  • Send an email to leanbytes0@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  • Include the email address associated with your account.
  • We will process your request and permanently delete all your personal data within 30 days of receiving your request.

Upon deletion, your name, email address, and profile photo will be permanently removed from our systems. This action is irreversible.

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function smoothly:

  • Notifications: Used to signal the end of a countdown or interval.
  • Exact Alarms: Necessary for precision timing, ensuring your intervals are accurate down to the millisecond.
  • Background Service: Allows the timer to continue running while you focus on other tasks or dim your screen.
  • Camera / Photo Library: Used to capture or select a profile photo during account creation.

Analytics & Ads

This app contains no advertising, no tracking SDKs, and no hidden analytics. Your time is yours alone.

Contact

If you have questions regarding our commitment to privacy or wish to exercise your data rights, reach out at: leanbytes0@gmail.com
