Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 15, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Bytes Developer handles your data when you use the Shizuku Game Booster app. We are committed to minimizing data collection and ensuring your privacy.

Data Collection & Advertising

Shizuku Game Booster does not require sign-up and does not collect personal information such as names, emails, or direct device identifiers for our own servers. However, the app is ad-supported and uses third-party advertising networks (like Google AdMob). These networks may collect anonymous usage data and unique advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads.

Permissions & System Access

To provide advanced configuration options, the app requires specific permissions:

  • Shizuku Service Access: Used to access hidden system APIs for toggling Do Not Disturb (DND), adjusting refresh rates, applying DNS profiles, scaling resolution, and forcing MSAA without requiring root access.
  • Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS): Used to detect when a game is launched or closed so the app can automatically apply or remove your gaming optimizations.
  • Overlay / System Alert Window: Used to display the real-time performance and FPS overlay over your active games.
  • Background App Control: Used to manage background tasks and ensure a clean device state before starting a game.
  • Internet Access: Required to apply custom DNS configurations and to load advertisements.

Data Security

Any optimizations made via Shizuku are executed locally on your device. We do not transmit your system settings, app usage statistics, or gaming preferences to any external servers.

Children's Privacy

Shizuku Game Booster is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our app's capabilities or legal requirements. Continued use of the app after changes are posted indicates your acceptance.
