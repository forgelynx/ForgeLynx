Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Photo Widget - Living Gallery. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how our application handles your data and what permissions we require to provide a premium widget experience.

Data Collection

We believe in a "Privacy First" approach. Photo Widget does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All processing happens locally on your device.

Permissions & Usage

To function correctly, the app requires the following permissions:

  • Gallery/Storage Access: Used exclusively to allow you to select photos from your device to display in widgets. We do not "read" your entire gallery; we only access the specific images you choose.
  • Schedule Exact Alarm: Required for the "Living Gallery" feature to update your home screen widgets at precise intervals (e.g., changing the photo every hour).
  • Background Processing: Ensures your widgets stay updated even when the app is not actively open in the foreground.

Third-Party Services

This app does not include third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs. Your interaction with the app remains completely private.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, this app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
