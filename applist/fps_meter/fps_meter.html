About the App

FPS Meter Real-time Display allows you to monitor the real-time frames per second (FPS) of your device anywhere on your screen. It's a lightweight and customizable tool designed for gamers and developers.

Note: This app displays your display's refresh rate, which is often equivalent to FPS in many scenarios, but it does not hook into the GPU to show game-specific FPS if the game is running at a lower frame rate than the screen refresh rate.
