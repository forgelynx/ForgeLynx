My Journey

My path as a solo developer began with a simple question: Why is modern software so noisy? I decided to spend my time building the opposite.

From the first line of code written in a late-night session to 10+ apps on the Play Store, my focus has remained constant: **High-precision digital craft.** I don't just 'make apps'—I build digital companions that respect your time and attention.

The Philosophy

  • 1

    Lean by Choice: Every feature must earn its place. If it doesn't add value, it adds noise.

  • 2

    Aesthetic Precision: Design isn't just how it looks; it's how it feels when you tap that button.

  • 3

    Privacy First: Your data belongs to you. Period. No exceptions, no tracking SDKs.

The Vision for Lean Bytes

I am currently focused on building a coherent ecosystem of productivity tools that work seamlessly together. From performance monitors like Shizuku FPS to utility gems like Photo Widget, the goal is to define a new standard for independent software.
